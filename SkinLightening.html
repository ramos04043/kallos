Skin Lightening Treatment in Chennai

Your skin reflects your lifestyle, environment, and overall health. Daily exposure to sun, pollution, dehydration, and stress can gradually lead to dullness, uneven tone, pigmentation, and premature skin damage. At Kallos Aesthetics, Chennai, we offer science-backed skin lightening treatments designed to repair damage, improve skin tone, and restore your natural radiance safely and effectively.

What Causes This Problem

  • Sun Damage & UV Exposure
    Prolonged exposure to high UV rays—especially common for outdoor workers, joggers, and seaside residents—can trigger excess melanin production, tanning, and pigmentation.

  • Dry & Dehydrated Skin
    Lack of moisture weakens the skin barrier, making skin appear rough, tired, and lifeless.

  • Environmental Damage
    Pollution, dust, and harsh climate conditions can lead to oxidative stress and skin dullness.

  • Cold Burns & Environmental Sensitivity
    Sudden temperature changes and environmental exposure can damage the skin surface, affecting tone and texture.

  • Lifestyle & Skincare Gaps
    Poor nutrition, stress, inadequate skincare, and aging slow down cell renewal, resulting in uneven skin tone.

Treatment Preparation & Aftercare

Before Treatment

  • Avoid direct sun exposure for 48 hours prior.
  • Discontinue retinol or strong exfoliants 3 days before.
  • Stay hydrated and arrive with a clean, makeup-free face.
  • Inform your dermatologist about any recent skin allergies.

After Care

  • Apply broad-spectrum sunscreen (SPF 50+) daily.
  • Use a gentle, non-soap cleanser for the first 2 days.
  • Avoid hot showers, saunas, or heavy sweating for 24 hours.
  • Use the prescribed moisturizer to maintain skin barrier health.
