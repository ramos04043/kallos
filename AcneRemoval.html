Acne is more than just a skin concern—it can affect confidence and leave long-term marks if not treated correctly. Factors such as hormonal imbalance, excess oil production, clogged pores, stress, diet, and environmental exposure often trigger pimples and acne breakouts. At Kallos Aesthetics, Chennai, we offer advanced, dermatologist-guided acne treatments that address both active acne and post-acne damage.

Our approach focuses on treating acne at the root, calming inflammation, preventing future breakouts, and restoring healthy skin texture.

What Causes Acne & Acne Scars?

Acne can occur at any age and may leave lasting marks if untreated:

  • Excess oil (sebum) production
  • Clogged pores and bacterial growth
  • Hormonal fluctuations
  • Stress and lifestyle factors
  • Improper skincare routines
  • Inflammation leading to acne scars and pigmentation
  • Over time, repeated breakouts can damage the skin's structure, resulting in scars, uneven texture, and dullness.
