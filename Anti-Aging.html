What Causes Skin Aging & Dullness?

As we age, the skin undergoes structural and functional changes:

Loss of Collagen & Elastin

Reduced collagen leads to sagging skin, fine lines, and loss of firmness.

Decreased Hydration

Lower hyaluronic acid levels cause dryness, rough texture, and tired-looking skin.

Sun Damage & Environmental Stress

UV exposure accelerates wrinkles, age spots, and pigmentation.

Under-Eye Aging

Thinning skin and fat loss result in eye bags, dark circles, and hollowness.

Oxidative Stress & Lifestyle Factors

Pollution, stress, poor sleep, and nutrition speed up premature aging.

Cellular Damage

Free radicals and environmental toxins damage skin cells, reducing their ability to repair and regenerate.
